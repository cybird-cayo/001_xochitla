CyberSec Adventures

At a high-level, I like to begin most projects by building out a lay of the land and understanding the types of technologies, processes and services I'll be working with. I start most projects by compiling different data points into a project repository that I can later parse for unique endpoints/data. My goal at this phase is to identify potential attack vectors, discover subdomains, and assess security vulnerabilities in a proactive manner.

  • 1. Subdomain Enumeration
    I use a variety of tools to perform automated and manual scans in search of unique endpoints.
  • 2. Subdomain Validation and Content Discovery
    I validate the unique endpoints and subdomains for activity and content using HTML response codes and response headers.
  • 3. Fingerprinting and Technology Identification
    I'll fingerprint the active server/endpoint to identify unique technologies being used.
  • 4. Testing and Bug Identification
    I will initiate testing once potential attack vectors are identified, evaluating against various bug categories such as: SQL Injection, Cross-Site Scripting, Information Disclosure.
